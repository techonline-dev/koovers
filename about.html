About Us

Koovers - India’s leading B2B quick commerce platform, revolutionizing the automotive aftermarket with seamless spare parts ordering and delivery. As a 100% subsidiary of Schaeffler India Ltd, Koovers is committed to ensuring that workshops and retailers receive the right parts at the right time, every time. With a vast network across India and an ever-growing customer base, we are the go-to solution for independent garages and retailers looking for efficiency, reliability, and quality.


Our platform provides a hassle-free experience for ordering OEM and OES-certified spare parts, ensuring superior performance and longevity. Backed by a robust logistics network and cutting-edge technology, Koovers is setting new standards in the automotive spare parts industry.

Our Vision

To lead India’s automotive aftermarket ecosystem with customer-centric, technology-driven solutions that ensure quality, convenience, and growth.

Our Mission

  • To provide quick and reliable access to genuine spare parts for independent workshops and retailers.
  • To enhance workshop productivity with our cutting-edge digital solutions.
  • To build a trusted network that ensures seamless and efficient operations for the automotive service industry.
  • To continuously innovate and adapt to the evolving needs of the automotive aftermarket.
