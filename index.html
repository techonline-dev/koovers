About Us

Koovers - India’s leading B2B quick commerce platform, revolutionizing the automotive aftermarket with seamless spare parts ordering and delivery. As a 100% subsidiary of Schaeffler India Ltd, Koovers is committed to ensuring that workshops and retailers receive the right parts at the right time, every time.


With a vast network across India and an ever-growing customer base, we are the go-to solution for independent garages and retailers looking for efficiency, reliability, and quality.
